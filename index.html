Welcome to the Quiz!

1. Choose Your Challenge:

Pick a topic and select your difficulty level. The difficulty determines your total time limit for the entire quiz:

  • Easy: 10 minutes — for a relaxed experience.
  • Medium: 7 minutes — for those who feel confident.
  • Hard: 5 minutes — a true test of speed and knowledge!

2. One Tap – One Answer:

Be careful! As soon as you click an answer option, it is recorded automatically. You cannot change your choice or go back to the previous question. You will be immediately redirected to the next question.

3. The Clock is Ticking:

The timer starts the moment you press the "Start" button and counts down for all 10 questions combined.

  • Keep an eye on the countdown in the corner of your screen.
  • If time runs out before you finish — the game will end automatically, and your current progress will be saved.

4. Your Score:

Your goal is to provide as many correct answers as possible within the time limit. Your final score will reveal how well you know the chosen topic!
